The Art of Permanent Makeup

Permanent Makeup (PMU) is an advanced cosmetic technique that uses specialized pigments to enhance your natural features. Unlike traditional tattoos, PMU is applied to the superficial layers of the skin, resulting in a soft, natural look that saves you time every day.

Anna Erkhova, PMU Master

Anna specializes in the modern "powder" and "ombre" techniques, which create a delicate, pixelated effect rather than harsh lines for effortlessly elegant and long-lasting results.

She honed her permanent makeup expertise in Moscow from 2016 to 2022, where she developed her signature approach combining classical art training with precision PMU techniques. Her background in sculpture and applied art informs every procedure, ensuring natural, elegant results tailored to each client's unique features.
