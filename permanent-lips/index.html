What Are Permanent Lips?

Permanent lips, often called lip blush or permanent lip color, are a form of PMU designed to enhance the lips with carefully placed pigment. The goal is not a flat or harsh tattoo effect, but a softer, more elegant result that improves the overall look of the lips once healed.

Depending on your preferences, the result can be kept very soft and understated or brought slightly more toward a polished, makeup-inspired finish. The right approach depends on your natural lip tone, facial features, and personal style.

Why Clients Choose Permanent Lips

For Beauty & Freshness

Permanent lips can bring back shape, softness, and balance to lips that feel pale, uneven, or less defined over time. For many clients, the result is a fresher face even without makeup.

For Daily Ease

If you do not want to rely on lipstick every day, permanent lips can make your overall look feel more complete from morning onward — convenient for workdays, active routines, and travel.

The Appointment Process

1. Consultation & Design Direction

We begin by discussing the desired look — from very natural tone restoration to a more defined and aesthetic finish.

2. Shape & Pigment Choice

Lip tone and shape are assessed carefully so the chosen direction suits your face and heals in a flattering way.

3. Procedure

A numbing product is used to improve comfort. Sensitivity varies, but most clients find the session manageable.

4. Healing & Touch-Up Session

Lip PMU is typically a two-step process. The first session creates the base, and a later touch-up session refines retention, tone, and overall finish.

Healing & Longevity

Immediately after treatment, the lips usually appear stronger in color than the final healed result. Some swelling is also normal in the early stage. As healing progresses, the color softens.

Final healed results develop over several weeks. Longevity depends on factors such as skin, lifestyle, and aftercare, but PMU lips are designed to provide a long-lasting improvement rather than a temporary daily cosmetic effect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my lips look too bright or artificial?

Not when the work is planned properly. Lip PMU can be extremely soft and natural, but it can also be designed to look more visibly polished if that is what you want. The key is choosing the right level for your face and taste.

Is this the same as wearing lipstick all the time?

No. Healed permanent lips are usually softer and more integrated into your natural features. The goal is long-lasting enhancement, not a flat cosmetic layer.

How long does the appointment take?

Usually around 1 to 1.5 hours, including consultation, preparation, numbing, and the procedure itself.

Can I send photos before booking?

Yes. If you want an initial opinion before booking, a digital consultation is a very practical way to start.
