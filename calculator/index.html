Time Savings Calculator

How much of your life goes into a routine that washes off at the end of the day? Adjust the sliders to find out.

Set how many minutes your brow and lip routine takes and how many days per week you do it. The calculator applies an 80% saving rate — the share of that time PMU removes from your day. Monthly figures use 4.3 weeks; yearly figures use 52 weeks. Results are estimates for illustration and will vary based on your routine and skin type.
